The pursuit of functional minimalism in digital interfaces is often mistaken for a lack of visual information. In reality, minimalism is the deliberate curation of structural elements to prioritize human cognition over decorative noise.

Fig. 1.1: The physical manifestation of Warm Minimalism.

When we design with "linen" textures and "logic" structures, we bridge the gap between the tactile comfort of paper and the efficiency of the digital screen. This approach minimizes eye strain while maintaining the clarity required for complex technical documentation.
